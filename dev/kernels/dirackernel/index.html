DiracKernel

The Dirac kernel with conditional mean prameter $\mu$ is denotd by

\[k(y\mid x) = \delta(y - \mu(x)).\]

Computes a Dirac kernel of eltype T from the Dirac kernel K if T and U are compatible. That is T and U must both be Real or both be Complex.
